Spiced Apple Cider

Ingredients

  • 8 cups apple cider
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 1 orange, cut into slices
  • 1 cup sweetened whipped cream

Directions

  • Prep: 10 min
  • Cook: 5 min
  • Servings: 8 cups
